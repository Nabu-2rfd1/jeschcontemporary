Lazy Notes of a Friday Afternoon

By Evans Haji-Touma, 2025

Graphite on notebook paper

Looping animated GIF (1×1) · graphite sketch & digital animation

Lazy Notes of a Friday Afternoon traces the idle choreography of graphite when thought wanders. The piece begins as casual scribbling—hesitations, overdrawn lines, erasures—then finds a rhythm, tiling into a quiet order before slipping back into the margins. The work keeps the tactility of pencil and paper while embracing digital repetition, inviting viewers to linger in the space between sketch and structure.

Medium: graphite & digital, looping GIF · Dimensions: 1×1 (responsive) · Duration: continuous loop
