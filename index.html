Friday Afternoon Call Notes

By Evans Haji-Touma, 2025

Graphite on notebook paper

Friday Afternoon Call Notes traces the idle choreography of graphite as attention loosens. Through patient repetition, incidental marks gather into motifs that feel discovered rather than designed. What begins as casual notation organizes itself, proposing a legibility that precedes language. The work lingers at the threshold between intention and drift, where pattern lets the subconscious surface without announcing itself.
