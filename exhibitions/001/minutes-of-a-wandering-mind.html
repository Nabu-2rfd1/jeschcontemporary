Minutes of a Wandering Mind

Minutes of a Wandering Mind represents a series of drawings that document the persistent struggle of the individual for self-expression within the rigid frameworks of today's corporate culture. Created during actual meetings and calls, they record the mind’s effort to escape a reductionist environment that demands thoughts to appear as bullet points and key metrics. The drawings keep the look of minutes while undoing their purpose, letting a deeper primordial order edge into view.
